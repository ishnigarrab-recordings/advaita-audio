# advaita-audio
Advaita Audio | Occult. Ritual. Music.

2006-Present

Founded in 2006 by Brian Artwick, Advaita Audio features [Split Channel], Dark Ambient, Industrial, and Experimental ritual audio releases.

[Split Channel] Releases:

Devised and originated by Advaita Audio, [split channel] releases consist of two artists playing simultaneously (one in the left channel and another in the right channel). The result is an auditory experience unique to Advaita Audio.
